ST PETERS NGOISA PARISH GROUPS

St Peters Ngoisa Parish is blessed with vibrant Catholic groups that strengthen faith, promote unity, and support the mission of the Church. Each group plays a unique and important role in building our parish community.

CMA

Catholic Men Association dedicated to spiritual growth, leadership, and service within the parish.

CWA

Catholic Women Association promoting faith, family values, and active participation in church life.

PMY

Pontifical Missionary Youth nurturing young Catholics in missionary spirit and evangelization.

PMC

Pontifical Missionary Children guiding children to grow in faith, prayer, and love for the Church.

JUMUIYA

Small Christian Communities that strengthen fellowship, prayer, and mutual support among families.

Choir

Leading the congregation in sacred music and enhancing liturgical celebrations.

Altar Servers

Assisting the priest during Mass and other liturgical celebrations with dedication and reverence.

Lectors

Proclaiming the Word of God during Mass and guiding the faithful in Scripture reflection.

We Are st peters ngoisa parish

We are a Catholic community. We worship, serve, and grow in faith together. We care for our parish, outstations, and each member.

Our Purpose

We guide members to live in faith, love, and service. We teach the Word of God and strengthen our community.

Our Mission

We share Christ’s love, support each member, and grow disciples. We aim to build strong, caring families in the church.

“For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life.”

John 3:16

Our Values

Identity in Christ

We live our identity as children of God and reflect His love.

Church Is Family

We treat each member as family, caring and supporting one another.

Kingdom Culture

We follow God’s ways, serving with humility and faithfulness.

Spirit Led Life

We live guided by the Holy Spirit in all our actions and decisions.

What We Believe

Faith in Christ

Jesus Christ is Lord. Through Him we have life and salvation.

Living Word

The Bible guides our life and choices. We obey its teachings.

Service

We serve God and others with love, humility, and generosity.

Community

We build strong families, care for the needy, and share God’s love.
